Understanding Your Purchase Taxes

Portugal's IMT (property transfer tax) uses progressive brackets. Primary residences get significant discounts at lower price points, but these benefits phase out as price increases.

The chart below shows exactly how much IMT discount you get at each price level — and where the discount phases to zero.
