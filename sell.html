Our promise

The 50/50 Partilha Guarantee.

When selling a home, some approaches focus on keeping the listing strictly within one agency's internal network. While this is a common practice, we believe in a fully open approach.

The In-House Approach

  • Limits your home's exposure to one agency's buyers
  • Relies on a smaller pool of potential offers

Our Collaborative Approach

  • 100% Market Exposure: We guarantee a 50/50 commission split with any licensed agent who brings a buyer
  • Maximum Competition: Your home is marketed to the entire real estate community, not just our internal network
  • Seller-First Focus: Faster sales and top-dollar results by maximizing your property's visibility

By ensuring every agent in Portugal has a financial incentive to show your home, we open the doors to every single buyer in the market.
