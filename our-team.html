CEO & Founding Partner

Angela Dhillon

With more than a decade of experience in real estate brokerage, Angela brings a highly strategic and client-centered approach to Aldeia Realty. A trained Speech and Language Therapist, she has a singular aptitude for navigating complex negotiations and delivering clear, transparent communication to international buyers entering the Portuguese market.

Before moving to the Silver Coast, Angela built a benchmark career in Greenville, North Carolina — as the founding agent of one of the region’s leading real estate firms. She set the firm’s standard for excellence, consistently ranking as the top-performing consultant year after year and winning the firm’s highest-value listings.

Today, Angela and her family are proud to call the Caldas da Rainha region home. Deeply passionate about Portuguese culture, she is actively engaged in the local community and dedicates herself to helping international newcomers integrate seamlessly into the lifestyle the Silver Coast has to offer.
