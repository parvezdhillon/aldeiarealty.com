Comprar Imóvel em Portugal

Sim, vai precisar de uma conta bancária portuguesa para completar a compra de um imóvel. A conta é utilizada parar the deposit (sinal), final payment, e ongoing utility direct debits e tax payments. Most Portuguese banks offer services in English e can open accounts for non-residents. You will typically need your passport, NIF, e proof of address. We can introduce you to relationship managers at trusted banks.

O NIF (Número de Identificação Fiscal) é o seu número de identificação fiscal português number. It is required for almost everything: opening a bank account, buying property, signing contracts, e setting up utilities. Pode obtain a NIF at any local Finanças office or through a lawyer/fiscal representative. Non-residents need a fiscal representative (we can recommend one). The process takes a few hours e costs around €10.

Da aceitação da oferta à escritura final, o processo tipicamente demora 2–4 meses. Este includes due diligence (2–4 weeks), signing the promissory contract (CPCV) with a 10% deposit, e scheduling the final deed at the Notary. Cash purchases can be faster (6–8 weeks), while mortgage purchases may take longer (3–5 months) depending on bank approval timelines.

Embora não seja legalmente obrigatório, recomendamos vivamente contratar um advogado independente para realizar due diligence on your behalf. A lawyer verifies property ownership, checks for outsteing debts or liens, confirms building licenses, e reviews all documentation. We work with several trusted lawyers on a Costa de Prata. Legal fees typically range from €800 to €1,500 plus VAT.

RNH & IMPOSTOS

Benefícios RNH & Fiscais

O regime de Residente Não Habitual (RNH) era um programa fiscal português que oferecia benefícios significativosits for up to 10 years, including a flat 20% tax rate on qualifying Portuguese income e exemptions on most foreign-sourced income. Atualização importante: o regime RNH original encerrado para novos candidatos a 1 de Janeiro de 2024. No entanto, várias categorias de pessoas podem still benefit:

  • Aqueles que já detêm estatuto RNH continuam a usufruir dos benefícios durante todo o seu período de 10 anos
  • Individuals who became Portuguese tax residents in 2023 e submitted their application by 31 Março 2024
  • Certain professionals moving to Portugal under specific work contracts
  • Nómadas digitais e trabalhadores remotos que se registaram ao abrigo do regime antes do prazo

O governo português introduziu o IFICI (Incentivo Fiscal para o Investimento, a Criação e a Inovação) regime as a partial replacement, offering a reduced 20% tax rate for qualifying high-value activities. We recommend consulting a Portuguese tax advisor for the latest rules specific to your situation. Leia o nosso Guia RNH completo →

Embora o regime RNH original já não esteja aberto a novos candidatos, Portugal ainda oferece regimes fiscais atractivostax structures:

  • IFICI regime: Um novo incentivo que oferece uma taxa fixa de 20% sobre rendimentos de trabalho dependente e independente de actividades qualificadashigh-value activities, including many professional, tech, e executive roles
  • Steard Portuguese tax residency: de Portugal steard progressive tax rates (14.5%–48%) may still be favourable compared to many countries, especially with de Portugal extensive double-taxation treaty network
  • Golden Visa holders: Aqueles que obtiveram residência por Visto Dourado antes das alterações de 2023 ainda podem ter NHR access depending on their timeline

Property buyers in Portugal pay two main taxes: IMT (Imposto Municipal sobre Transmissões Onerosas de Imóveis) — a progressive property transfer tax ranging from 0% to 7.5% depending on the property value, location, e purpose (primary residence vs. secondary); e Stamp Duty (Imposto de Selo) — a flat 0.8% of the property price. For primary residences, significant IMT discounts e exemptions apply for properties under €101,917 (mainle). Use our Calculadora de Impostos for an exact breakdown.

Rental income from Portuguese property is taxed at a flat rate of 28%, regardless of whether you are under NHR, IFICI, or the steard tax regime. Pode deduct certain expenses (maintenance, management fees, insurance, IMI property tax) to reduce the taxable amount. Alternatively, pode opt to include rental income in your general progressive tax rate if that resultados in a lower liability. We recommend speaking with an accountant to determine the optimal approach for your situation.

IMI (Imposto Municipal sobre Imóveis) is de Portugal annual property tax, paid to the local council. Rates vary by municipality e property type, typically ranging from 0.3% to 0.8% of the property's taxable patrimonial value (VPT). On a Costa de Prata, most municipalities charge between 0.3% e 0.5%. The VPT is often significantly lower than the market value, so the actual tax bill is usually quite modest.

LIVING IN PORTUGAL

Life on a Costa de Prata

The Costa de Prata is significantly more affordable. On average: groceries 30–40% less, dining out 40–50% cheaper, services 20–30% less, e healthcare 60–70% more affordable. Property costs 40–60% less than comparable coastal areas in the US or UK. A comfortable lifestyle for a couple typically costs €2,000–€2,500 per month including rent or mortgage, utilities, groceries, e dining out.

The Costa de Prata enjoys a mild Mediterranean climate with over 300 days of sunshine per year. Summers are warm e dry (25–32°C), while winters are mild e damp (8–15°C). The Atlantic breeze keeps summers comfortable, e the region rarely experiences temperature extremes. It is an ideal climate for outdoor living year-round.

Portugal has a high-quality public healthcare system (SNS) that is free or low-cost for residents. Many expats also choose private health insurance for faster access to specialists, with comprehensive plans starting from around €50–€100 per month. The Costa de Prata has excellent public e private hospitals in Caldas da Rainha, Leiria, e Lisbon (1 hour away). Healthcare in Portugal consistently ranks among the best in the world.

The good news: Portuguese people are patient e encouraging with learners, e there are excellent language schools e tutors on a Costa de Prata.
