OUR PROMISE

What You Can Expect

Guaranteed 50/50 Partilha

No games, no hidden administrative deductions, and no sliding scales. We guarantee a clean 50/50 split on the total commission, backed by a signed Protocolo de Partilha.

Frictionless Documentation

We keep a complete, updated property file (Caderneta Predial, Certidao Permanente, Licenca de Utilizacao, and Certificado Energetico) ready for every listing. You won't have to chase us for documents.

Client Protection

We strictly respect the AMI licensing rules and the agent-buyer relationship. Your clients remain your clients. We manage the seller; you manage the buyer.

CPCV to Escritura Support

Our team works collaboratively with you and the Solicitador or Notary to ensure the Contrato de Promessa de Compra e Venda is drafted smoothly and the final Escritura (Deed) crosses the finish line without delays.
